Timesheet CSV to PDF Filler

Upload your CSV and PDF files to generate a filled timesheet.

Instructions:

  1. Prepare your CSV file with format: date,time_range,hours (e.g., '9/1/24,11:15 - 5:00,5.75')
  2. Use 'X' for days off (e.g., '9/1/24,X,X')
  3. Add your name as the last line of the CSV file
  4. Upload your CSV file in the first input
  5. Upload the Timesheet-Fillable.pdf in the second input
  6. Click "Process CSV and Fill PDF"
  7. Download the filled PDF when ready
